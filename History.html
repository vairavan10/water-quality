Water Pollution Statistics

Recent surveys and studies have revealed alarming trends:

  • Percentage of rivers polluted: 48%
  • Percentage of lakes polluted: 32%
  • Percentage of coastal waters polluted: 62%

These statistics highlight the urgent need for action to address water pollution globally.

Impact of Water Pollution

Water pollution poses significant threats to ecosystems and human health:

  • Contaminated water can lead to the spread of waterborne diseases such as cholera and typhoid.
  • Pollution harms aquatic life, disrupting ecosystems and threatening biodiversity.
  • Toxic chemicals in water can accumulate in the food chain, posing risks to human health.

Addressing water pollution is crucial for safeguarding public health and protecting the environment.
